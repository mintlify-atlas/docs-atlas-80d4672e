## What HireFlow does

HireFlow eliminates the opacity of modern hiring. When a candidate applies, their resume is automatically parsed, embedded, and scored against the job description. Recruiters see a ranked list of applicants with match scores and skill breakdowns — no manual sifting required.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register as a job seeker or recruiter. Job seekers complete a brief onboarding survey about their preferences and target roles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your resume or post a job">
    Job seekers upload a PDF or DOCX resume — HireFlow's AI pipeline parses it instantly. Recruiters post job descriptions with requirements and salary ranges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get AI-powered insights">
    Job seekers receive an ATS score and a list of matched jobs ranked by fit. Recruiters see an AI-ranked candidate list with match score breakdowns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track the full pipeline">
    Every application moves through a transparent kanban pipeline. Candidates are notified at each stage — no more wondering where you stand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tech stack at a glance

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Frontend | Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS, Zustand |
| Backend | FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Celery |
| AI | OpenAI GPT-4o-mini + text-embedding-3-small |
| Vector DB | pgvector (dev) / Pinecone (prod) |
| Database | SQLite (dev) / PostgreSQL (prod) |
| Queue | Redis + Celery |
| Storage | Local (dev) / AWS S3 (prod) |
