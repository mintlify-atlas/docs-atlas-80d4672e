KAREN (Knowledge-Augmented Recruitment and Employment Navigator) is HireFlow's built-in AI agent. For job seekers, KAREN acts as a direct, data-driven career coach — no filler phrases, no false encouragement. She has access to your resume, ATS score, skills, and application history, so her feedback is grounded in your actual profile.

## What KAREN can do

- Review your resume and identify specific weaknesses
- Explain your ATS score and what's dragging it down
- Identify skills missing from your profile relative to a target role
- Show you your strongest job matches and explain why
- **Apply to jobs on your behalf** when you ask her to
- Suggest follow-up actions based on your current applications

<Note>
  KAREN can auto-apply to jobs when you explicitly ask. For all other actions — withdrawing applications, updating your profile, changing preferences — she will suggest what to do but will not act without you doing it yourself.
</Note>

## Starting a conversation

Send a message to KAREN using the chat endpoint:

```bash
curl -X POST http://localhost:8000/api/v1/karen/chat \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <token>" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"message": "What is my ATS score and how do I improve it?"}'
```

KAREN responds with a reply, an optional `action_taken` field (if she performed an action like applying to a job), and follow-up suggestions:

```json
{
  "reply": "Your ATS score is 58 — grade D. The main issues are low keyword density (22/35) and a multi-column layout that most parsers choke on. Specifically, you're missing: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD. These appear in over 70% of backend engineering JDs. Fix the layout first — it's costing you 8 points on its own.",
  "action_taken": null,
  "suggestions": [
    "What's my ATS score?",
    "What skills am I missing for my target role?",
    "Show me jobs matching my profile",
    "How do I improve my resume?"
  ]
}
```

## Example conversation

Here's a realistic example of using KAREN to prepare for a specific role:

**You:** I'm targeting senior backend roles at fintech companies. What's holding my resume back?

**KAREN:** Your ATS score is 62 — passable, but you're leaving points on the table. Three problems worth fixing:

1. You list "payment systems" twice but never mention PCI-DSS compliance — that's table stakes for fintech.
2. Your skills section has 23 items crammed in one line. Parsers see that as a wall of text. Break it into categories.
3. No quantified achievements in your last two roles. "Improved system performance" means nothing. "Reduced p99 latency from 800ms to 120ms" does.

Fix those three things and you'll likely clear 75.

---

**You:** Apply to the Senior Backend Engineer role at Acme Corp for me.

**KAREN:** Done.

```json

  "suggestions": [
    "What's my ATS score?",
    "Show me jobs matching my profile"
  ]

```

## Role-aware responses

KAREN adapts her style based on your account role:

- **Job seekers** get direct, sometimes sarcastic career coaching rooted in your resume data, ATS score, and application history.
- **Recruiters** get analytical pipeline insights and candidate comparisons — a different mode entirely.

<Info>
  Premium users receive more detailed coaching responses, including more granular breakdown analysis and access to JD-specific ATS scoring context.
</Info>

## Conversation memory

KAREN remembers your previous conversations. She maintains a rolling window of recent messages and automatically summarizes older exchanges to keep context relevant without growing unbounded.

Memory is per-user and persists across sessions. When you start a new conversation, KAREN already knows your history.

**Memory limits:**
- KAREN keeps the last 10 messages in full
- When the conversation exceeds 15 messages, older messages are summarized and appended to a running summary
- The summary is injected into every new conversation as context

### View your memory

```bash
curl http://localhost:8000/api/v1/karen/memory \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <token>"
```

```json
{
  "total_messages": 24,
  "recent_messages": [
    { "role": "user", "content": "What's my ATS score?" },
    { "role": "assistant", "content": "Your ATS score is 68 — grade C. ..." }
  ],
  "has_summary": true
}
```

### Clear your memory

If you want KAREN to start fresh — for example, after a major resume overhaul — you can wipe her memory entirely:

```bash
curl -X DELETE http://localhost:8000/api/v1/karen/memory \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <token>"
```

```json
{
  "message": "Memory cleared. KAREN has forgotten everything."
}
```

<Warning>
  Clearing memory is permanent. KAREN will lose all context about your previous conversations and coaching sessions. You'll need to re-introduce your goals and situation in your next message.
</Warning>
