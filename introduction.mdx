HireFlow replaces opaque hiring systems with an AI-powered platform where every decision is explained. Job seekers see exactly why their resume scored the way it did, which jobs match their profile, and where they stand in the hiring pipeline — in real time. Recruiters get AI-ranked candidates, a full kanban pipeline, and instant notifications without manual back-and-forth.

## Key capabilities

- **ATS scoring** — Every uploaded resume is parsed and scored against industry criteria, with a detailed breakdown of strengths and gaps so candidates know exactly what to improve.
- **Semantic job matching** — Resume embeddings are compared against job embeddings using vector similarity, surfacing roles that genuinely match a candidate's background rather than keyword frequency.
- **Full pipeline tracking** — Applications move through defined stages (applied → ATS screening → interviews → offer → selected) and both sides see every transition in real time.
- **Karen AI assistant** — A conversational AI built on GPT-4o-mini that answers candidate questions, provides resume tips, and remembers context across sessions.
- **Candidate benchmarking** — Candidates are scored relative to the selected pool for a role, giving recruiters a clear signal and giving job seekers honest positioning data.
- **Resume optimizer** — Premium tier feature that rewrites resume sections to better match a target job description, powered by GPT-4o-mini.
- **Referral network** — Job seekers can request referrals from insiders at companies they are applying to, with a structured flow that protects both parties.

<Note>
